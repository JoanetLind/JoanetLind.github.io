    • To directly print a document from your laptop at Hz go to: https://hz.mynetpay.nl. Then click on Webprint, select your file and repeat the steps if you need to add more files. Click on advanced option to select the options you want. Now click on upload and your document will be ready to be retrieved. Register on the multifunctional printer by using your HZ pass on the TouchID and select "Print". Select the printer to which you have requested the print. Make sure there is enough credit on your printing account as only then the print will be completed. When your document is printed out, press "Stop" on the TouchID and then "Logout".
